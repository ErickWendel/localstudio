# Videos and GIFs

Videos and GIFs are media layers that can play inside the canvas and during presentation.

## GIFs

- Insert GIFs from configured GIPHY search.
- Toggle GIF playback from the media controls.
- Arrange and animate GIFs like other elements.

## Videos

- Import or replace video assets.
- Control playback behavior from the movie inspector.
- Use movie-start animation to coordinate video playback with slide builds.

## Presenter Behavior

Media playback should stay connected to presenter flow, so video and GIF behavior matters when a deck moves from editing to delivery.
