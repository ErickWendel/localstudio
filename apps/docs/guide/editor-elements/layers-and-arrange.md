# Layers and Arrange

Layer controls determine what appears above or below other objects on a slide.

## Arrange Controls

- Send to back.
- Bring to front.
- Move backward.
- Move forward.
- Align to horizontal center, vertical center, or page center.

## Locking And Visibility

Layer controls can prevent accidental edits and help focus on complex slides.
